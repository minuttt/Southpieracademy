About Us

Forged in Singapore,
Built to Dominate

South Pier Academy was founded with a singular vision: to elevate Singapore basketball to the highest level across Southeast Asia. Our name draws from the strength of the pier — unyielding, steadfast, and a gateway to greater horizons.

With a roster spanning Professional, Men's Division, U18, and U16 categories, we provide a complete developmental pathway from youth fundamentals to elite-level competition. Our warrior ethos — Discipline, Focus, Grit — is ingrained in every player who wears the South Pier jersey.
